Publishing...

This page indicates the site publish step. In most setups you can safely replace this file or remove it once your site is live.

Open site (index)

If your host expected a publish.html file, upload this file to your site root alongside index.html and your assets. After upload, open your site URL (or click "Open site") and verify index.html loads.

Troubleshooting tips:

  • Ensure index.html is present in the same directory.
  • Clear CDN or browser cache if you don't see updates.
  • Check your hosting provider's required entry filename (some expect index.html, not publish.html).
